The Epic Journey

The Tragedy Begins

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons, leaving only his sister Nezuko alive - but transformed into a demon herself.

Joining the Demon Slayer Corps

Determined to avenge his family and find a cure for Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, an organization dedicated to protecting humanity from the demon threat.

Allies and Adversaries

Throughout his journey, Tanjiro encounters powerful allies like Zenitsu and Inosuke, faces terrifying demons, and trains under the guidance of the Hashira - the most elite Demon Slayers.

The Ultimate Confrontation

The story builds toward an epic confrontation with Muzan Kibutsuji, the first demon and progenitor of all demons, in a battle that will determine the fate of humanity.

Epic Train Combat

The Mugen Train movie revolutionized action animation with its dynamic train-top battles. Ufotable's signature blend of 2D character animation and 3D CGI environments creates seamless, high-octane combat sequences that flow across moving trains and through collapsing carriages.

Emotional Depth & Impact

Beyond spectacular fights, Mugen Train delivers profound emotional weight. The animation masterfully conveys Rengoku's unwavering spirit, Tanjiro's determination, and the heartbreaking sacrifice that defines the film's legacy through subtle facial expressions and powerful visual storytelling.
